# Fee Flow

## Crowdfunding

Successful withdrawals:

- organizer receives raised amount minus fee
- treasury receives treasury share of fee
- staking receives staking share of fee in the same asset used by the campaign

## Launchpad

Successful settlement:

- project receives raised amount minus fee
- treasury receives treasury share of fee
- staking receives staking share of fee in the same asset used by the sale

## Staking

Rewards are routed into:

- XLM reward pool
- USDC reward pool

No mixed reward pool is used in v1.
